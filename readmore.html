About Us

We are a group of passionate and determined people who want to make a difference and who deeply believe that every child education matters.The world today faces an unprecedented need for new innovators, thinkers, and problem solving leaders.
Our goal is to create engaging, affordable, and powerful solutions that immerse students in STEM through the excitement of building and programming educational robotics kits. Robokalam is on a mission to bring tomorrow’s education here today. We are at the brink of really exciting technologies which we should embrace and share. The Engineers and Scientists of the INDIA have always been at the forefront of technology and so why not the students?

ASHAER Labs gives young people the chance to discover the wide world of robotics through Designing , Building and Learning. These 3 concepts make for a really rich playground for learning. The students work in pairs to overcome challenges and build extraordinary machines. They then sharpen their wits and program the machines to play their challenges against each other. It is a place where we collaborate, we laugh, we learn and we compete.

Our Vision

ASHAER Labs envision a world where every student has the opportunity to be inspired by the excitement of hands-on STEM learning and knows the feeling of creating something with technology. We want people of all ages and backgrounds to that creative problem solving is fun and see its importance in shaping a better future.

Why 21st Century Labs of Robokalam ?

In an age of Robots, IOT & AI, schools & colleges are teaching our children to be redundant. Robotics is gaining momentum in many schools in INDIA as well as worldwide. And while it's important to note that teachers who administer courses in this area should advanced knowledge in programming and other skills, students are reaping huge benefits, not just for the present, but also for the foreseeable AI future.
